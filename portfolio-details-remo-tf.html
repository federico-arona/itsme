The project

As a Game Engineer on "Remothered: Tormented Fathers" I was privileged to contribute to an indie video game that paid homage to classic horror aesthetics and mechanics.

This hauntingly captivating title presented me with a unique opportunity to blend my technical expertise with a deep appreciation for old-style horror gaming, resulting in a memorable player experience that embraced the essence of fear and suspense.

The indie nature of "Remothered: Tormented Fathers" allowed me to wear multiple hats, extending my responsibilities beyond traditional game engineering.

Some of the features I personally worked on are:

  • Development of core game functionalities such as Gameplay Progression, Save system, Interactions, collectibles.
  • AI Development using Behaviour Trees, Navigation System, AI Perception and EQS
  • Integration with Steam Online Services, XBOX Online Services. This included Achievements, Stats, Presences and Auth.
  • Porting to consoles (XboxOne, PS4) optimizing code and integrating with Microsoft and Sony SKDs

    • I engaged in level design and optimization, ensuring that each corridor, room, and corner exuded an air of chilling authenticity. Additionally, I actively participated in the integration of audio and visual effects that intensified the game's eerie ambiance.

    This multi-faceted role enabled me to collaborate directly with the team, fostering an environment of creativity and innovation as we collectively worked to resurrect the nostalgia of horror gaming's golden era.
