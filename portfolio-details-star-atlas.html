The project

In my role as a Senior Game Engineer at Star Atlas, I was immersed in the exciting world of an AAA metaverse video game featuring blockchain integrations.

Operating within the realm of Unreal Engine 5, I was tasked to craft an unparalleled player experience.

This involves optimizing performance, refining gameplay mechanics, and seamlessly integrating blockchain elements, all while capitalizing on Unreal Engine 5's graphical power.

Within the Star Atlas ecosystem, I collaborated with a skilled team world wide based shaping the future of interactive entertainment.

The real innovation lies in the integration of blockchain, granting players true ownership of in-game assets and nurturing a player-driven economy.

This groundbreaking project empowers me to push the envelope of what gaming can achieve. As a Senior Game Engineer, I find immense satisfaction in the synthesis of cutting-edge game development and revolutionary blockchain integration, all geared toward creating a metaverse that promises to reshape the gaming landscape.
