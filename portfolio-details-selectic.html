The project

At the forefront of Selectic's transformative journey, I am currently leading the development of a groundbreaking web app that is redefining the hiring process.

Operating as both a tech lead and a hands-on developer, I am harnessing the capabilities of Java Spring, MySQL, and MongoDB databases to architect a robust back-end infrastructure.
Seamlessly integrating this with Angular's dynamic front-end and orchestrating connections with Google Drive and Google Sheets, I am creating a comprehensive solution that not only addresses intricate business challenges but also reshapes how recruitment is approached.

In my role as a tech lead, I am steering a close-knit group of skilled developers to collectively shape the project's trajectory.

Collaborating closely with stakeholders, I am meticulously designing the app's architecture to ensure seamless compatibility with Google's suite of tools, alongside efficient data management using MySQL and MongoDB.

This amalgamation of technologies results in a dynamic and responsive user experience, while also fostering secure and streamlined integrations.
